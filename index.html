ICT Reflection


I am Kurt Rohan C. Villarta and this is my 1st to 4th Quarter Portfolio.

My reflection for 2nd Quarter: So far, ICT Portfolios are hard to do without a smudge of creativity. So I based mine on my all time favorite anime : HAIKYUU!! Which also resuscitated my love for Volleyball. And being only 5'6, I was inspired by Hinata Shoyo which helped my feel better and made me want to increase my vertical jump to be as cool as him.
Anyway, learning coding through creativity is another journey. Whether it's finding out how to use the -webkit-text-stroke property to put an outline like as you can see on the "ICT REFLECTION" text to make it look just like the text Haikyuu!! uses in its manga covers, or finding out how to use fading cells to add a hint of coolness to my website, I am surprised how far the journey of Coding has brought me. And with the new and upcoming lessons, I hope to be able to use more to simplify and add more complex designs.
